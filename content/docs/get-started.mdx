## Quick Start Guide

### For Innovators

1. **Sign Up**

   - Visit deonhub.tz
   - Click "Join as Innovator"

2. **Create Your Project**

   - Click "New Project"
   - Describe your idea
   - Set project details

3. **Connect**
   - Review developer applications
   - Choose developer
   - Start collaboration

### For Developers

1. **Create Profile**

   - Join at deonhub.tz
   - Click "Join as Developer"
   - Add your skills and experience

2. **Find Projects**
   - Browse available public projects
   - Apply to matching opportunities
   - Discuss with innovators

## Platform Features

- **Project Dashboard**: Manage your work
- **Collaboration**: Real-time communication
- **File Sharing**: Collaborate on documents
- **Task Tracking**: Monitor progress
